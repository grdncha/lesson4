Vibe Legend

Ukrainian Professional Dance Crew

About Us

We are one of the largest dance teams in Ukraine, competing in the MegaCrew Pro category. Our team consists of 40 unique, talented, and diverse dancers. We specialize in a wide range of styles, including jazz-funk, dancehall, female, choreography and hip-hop.

Over the years, we have participated in numerous competitions, festivals, and dance events, constantly pushing our limits and growing together. Our goal is not only to achieve success on stage but also to inspire others, share our passion for dance, and create unforgettable moments for our audience.
